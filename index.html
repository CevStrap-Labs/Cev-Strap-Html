Welcome to CevStrap

Welcome to CevStrap, the ultimate tool for customizing and enhancing your Roblox experience. Whether you're a developer, tester, or just a Roblox enthusiast, CevStrap offers a wide range of features to make your Roblox sessions more flexible and powerful.

Features

  • Custom Roblox Installation: Install Roblox in a custom directory (CustomRoblox) on your system.
  • FastFlags Management: Add, remove, and manage FastFlags to enable or disable experimental features in Roblox.
  • Multi-Instance Support: Launch multiple instances of Roblox simultaneously.
  • Roblox Studio Integration: Launch Roblox Studio directly from the CevStrap interface.
  • Customization Options: Customize the appearance of your Roblox client with personalized colors for the background, text, and buttons.
  • File Management: Locate and copy Roblox installation files from your system's AppData directory.
  • Configuration Saving: Save your FastFlags and customization settings to a configuration file.
  • Logging and Feedback: View real-time logs of all actions performed in the application.

How to Use

  1. Install Roblox: Use the "Install Roblox" tab to install Roblox in the CustomRoblox directory.
  2. Manage FastFlags: Add or remove FastFlags in the "FastFlags" tab to customize your Roblox client.
  3. Launch Multi-Instance: Use the "Multi-Instance" tab to launch multiple Roblox instances simultaneously.
  4. Customize Colors: Personalize your Roblox client's appearance in the "Customization" tab.
  5. Launch Roblox Studio: Access Roblox Studio directly from the "Roblox Studio" tab.
  6. Locate and Copy Files: Use the "Locate Files" and "Copy Files" tabs to manage your Roblox installation files.

System Requirements

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or later
  • Python: Python 3.8 or later (if running from source)
  • Disk Space: At least 500 MB of free space for Roblox installation and additional files
